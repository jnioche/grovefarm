Where to eat


  The Cock Inn
  Blakeney (2 miles)
Tel: (01594) 510 239
 

The Cock Inn is a 16th Century coaching inn situated on the edge of the picturesque Forest of Dean.

Restaurant times:
Kitchen closed on Monday
Tuesday to Saturday 12 noon - 2.30pm, 6pm-9pm
Sunday 12 noon - 2.30pm

  The Red Hart Inn
  Blaisdon (6 miles)
Tel: (01452) 830477
 

The Red Hart Inn has an excellent selection of home cooked food made with fresh vegetables. To accompany the meal there is also an interesting selection of wines from around the world. It also has a separate garden, childrens play area and beer terrace.

  The George
  Newnham (2 miles)
 

The George Cafe serves light lunches, teas and coffees. It is run by Oaklands Park and uses local and organic produces, including cheese made using our own milk.
